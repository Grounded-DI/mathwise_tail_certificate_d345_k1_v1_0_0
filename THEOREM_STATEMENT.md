# Theorem Statement

## Fixed exact-conductor theorem for \(D=\{3,4,5\}\), \(k=1\)

Let

\[
B=\{3^a:a\ge 1\}\cup\{4^b:b\ge 1\}\cup\{5^c:c\ge 1\}.
\]

Let \(\operatorname{SubSum}(B)\) denote the set of all finite subset sums of distinct elements of \(B\).

Then

\[
[80,\infty)\subseteq \operatorname{SubSum}(B)
\]

and

\[
79\notin \operatorname{SubSum}(B).
\]

Consequently, the exact fixed-case conductor is

\[
\boxed{C(\{3,4,5\},1)=80.}
\]

## Scope

This is a fixed-case certificate for \(D=\{3,4,5\}\), \(k=1\). It does not claim a general BEGL theorem, does not claim results for other triples \(D\), and does not claim results for \(k\ne 1\).
